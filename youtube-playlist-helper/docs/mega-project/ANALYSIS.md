# Competitive Analysis - YouTube Playlist Management

## Market State
Most YouTube playlist managers focus on:
- Grouping playlists into folders.
- Sorting/Filtering by duration or activity.
- Bulk operations.

## Key Competitors
- **PocketTube**: The leader in grouping and sidebar integration. Offers nested folders, watched filters, and length calculation.
- **YouTube's Native Queue/Playlist**: Minimal features, hard to manage large lists, no de-duplication, no bulk move across playlists.

## Gap Analysis
What's missing (The "Mega-Project" Opportunity):
- **Truly Advanced De-duplication**: Most only check Video ID. We can check content similarity, title/channel matches, and suggest alternatives for deleted videos.
- **Intelligent "ID Cards"**: Per-video metadata (notes, ratings, AI-generated summaries/tags) that stays with the video across playlists.
- **Undo/Redo System**: Most extensions have destructive operations with no easy "Undo".
- **Modular Dashboard**: A "next-level" GUI that feels like a professional system management tool, not just a browser popup.
- **Cloud Sync & Portability**: Freedom from browser storage limits via WebDAV or Google Drive.
- **AI Integration**: Auto-categorization, curriculum generation, and smart renaming.
